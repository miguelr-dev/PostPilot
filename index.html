How it works

  1. Paste your materialA meeting transcript, voice memo transcription, or rough notes — anything in your own words.
  2. Pick your settingsTone, length, number of variants, and how far back to look for news.
  3. We do the readingFresh AI news is gathered and matched against your themes and opinions.
  4. Copy and postReview the drafts, grab the image, paste into LinkedIn. Done.
